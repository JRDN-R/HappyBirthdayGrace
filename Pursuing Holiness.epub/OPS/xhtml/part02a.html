Discussion Questions

1. Why do you think a person might become legalistic?

2. How can we avoid our holiness turning into legalism?

3. Why is moral law important and what is its connection to holiness?

4. How can we say God is both absolute in His holiness and yet a God of love?

5. Paul described guidelines for Christian liberty concerning the eating of meat. What would be a modern-day example of a similar principle, and what would be the overarching guidelines for addressing the situation?
