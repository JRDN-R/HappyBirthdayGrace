Discussion Questions

1. Multiple principles must be considered when choosing apparel. Describe your process for deciding if clothes fit within your commitment to holiness.

2. Do you think apparel and adornment send a message to people around us? If so, how should that factor into the choices we make about dress?

3. How is hair a sign of glory?

4. Describe how hair reflects our relationship with God and within the family.

5. What do you think your apparel, adornment, and hair testify to the people around you?
